KinderMiner™

KinderMiner™ is a simple and fast text mining tool for filtering and ranking a list of target terms by their association with a key phrase. For example, KinderMiner™ can be used to rank a list of transcription factors (the target terms) by their association with embryonic stem cells (the key phrase), in order to find what transcription factors are important to the embryonic stem cell state.
